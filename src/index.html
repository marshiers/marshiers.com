In 2010, I laid my hands on a computer for the first time.


A monstrous Dell laptop armed with a fresh install of Windows 7. It became an absolute fixation for me.


Over fourteen years later, after completing several phases of education, and breaking many a laptop, I now study computer science at the University of Technology Sydney, majoring in data science and artificial intelligence.


My passions lie in high-performance and scientific computing, alongside the occasional dabbling in web development. I view perfection in software as gorgeous interfaces that complement seamless functionality, and I strive to channel that through my work.


I don't maintain an active social media presence, so feel free to contact me via email if need be.
